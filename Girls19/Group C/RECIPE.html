Recipe for jollof and chicken

  • ginger

  • oil
  • onga shrimp cube
  • chicken

  1. wash ginger,onion,garlic,peel and grind
  2. wash chicken and add some of the grinded spices
  3. light fire and place chicken to steam
  4. after 20 mins fry chicken in oil
  5. place pot on fire and add oil,slice onion to fry
  6. add tin tomatoes and add grinded spices
  7. stir for complete mixture,add sliced tomatoes and stir
  8. allow to boil for 2 mins and add some little water to boil for sometime
  9. add salt and pepper for taste in a reasonable quantity and stir, cover for some time and add water in a reasonable quantity and cover
