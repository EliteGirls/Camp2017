GRAVITY

Gravity is a force which pulls us towards the earth, eg.the force, which makes a mango and some other fruits drop from a tree.

NEWTON'S LAW OF GRAVITATION

Sir Isaac Newton made a study on the forces between bodies. He made a conclusion that:

  1. Any two object in the universe attract one another
  2. The force becomes less when the object become more distant from each other
  3. The magnitude of the force depends on the magnitude of their masses.
