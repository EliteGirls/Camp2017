MAGNETISM

Magnetic force is the force used in magnets to attract magnetic substances.
Magnetic force exists between
a.Magnets
b.Current carrying conductors
c.Magnetic substances and magnets.

Magnetic feilds

A permanent magnect wall exsert a force on a peice of of iron or another magnet some distance away.
The space surrounding a magnet in which magnetic force is exserted is called a Magnetic feild.
Induce magnetism is the process of magnetising an object made of magnetic material simply by bringing a magnet near.
