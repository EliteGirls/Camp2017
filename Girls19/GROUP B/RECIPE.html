How to prepare rice

Method
Health Benefits

Rice is easy to cook.

Ingredients

  • a cup of rice.
  • one and half cup of water.
  • a salt to taste.

Method

  1. Put a saucepan on fire.
  2. Add a little salt to give it a taste
  3. Pour one and half cup of water into saucepan
  4. Wait to boil
  5. Pour the one cup of rice into the boiling water
  6. Stir a little and cover.
  7. Wait for about 45minutes
  8. Remove from fire
  9. Food is ready to eat with anything of choice
