Recipe for Jollof and chicken

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice

  • Vegetable oil

  • Fresh tomatoes

  • onion

  • canned tomatoes

  • powdered pepper

  • fresh chicken

  • Ginger

  • Garlic

  • maggie

  • green pepper

  • salt

METHOD

  1. wash ginger,garlic,onion and fresh pepper with brine

  2. peel and cut into smaller chops

  3. blend with the blender

  4. Cut chicken and season with maggie,salt and blended ingredients

  5. steam for a while and fry the chichen

  6. slice onions and tomatoes and fry with canned tomatoes in saucepan till thesauce is ready

  7. add stock ,salt,powdered pepperand maggie

  8. steam for a while with little water

  9. add washed rice in the right proportion and add enough water and stirr

  10. cook till rice is ready
