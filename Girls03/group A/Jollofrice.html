HOW JOLLOF IS PREPARED

This website teaches you how to prepare a rice meal known as Jollofrice.

Go to Conclusion

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  • Meat
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Spices

RECIPE/INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Chop tomatoes and onions
  2. Wash your meat and season with blended garlic and ginger
  3. Pour oil into saucepan and add chopped onions
  4. Wait for a few minuites and add chopped tomatoes to the onions in the saucepan
  5. Grind pepper and add to the ingredients in the saucepan
  6. Add seasoned meat to the ingredients in the saucepan and wait for about ten minutes
  7. Add rice and later add water

NUTRIENT/BENEFITS

  • Jollof rice contains carbohydratesfrom the rice
  • It also contains protein from the meat
  • It contains fats and oil
