Recipe:Jollof rice and Fried chicken

Preparation of Jollof rice and Fried chicken

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Tin tomatoes
  • Onions
  • pepper(optional)
  • chicken
  • salt to taste
  • water
  • spices
  • tomatoes

Method

  1. Slice all vegetables and wash them and place them aside in a bowl
  2. In a saucepan placed over heat, pour a little amount of oil and leave the oil to heat up
  3. Add your sliced onion to your heated oil in the saucepan and leave to saute
  4. Add pepper and the vegetabes along side with the fresh tomatoes
  5. Then add the tin tomatoes to the vegetables in the saucepan until it becomes a sauce
  6. Add the spices and salt to the sauce to taste
  7. Now add your clean rice to the sauce and stir then finally add water to it
  8. Cover the saucepan and leave rice to boil for about 40 minutes
  9. Wash your chicken and put it in a saucepan together with onions, pepper and spices
  10. Put the chicken on heat and allow it to boil for about 15 minutes
  11. Take the chicken off the heat once it is boiled and strain the stock and add it to the boiling rice
  12. In a frying pan, place some oil and allow it to get hot
  13. Gently place the chicken into the heated oil and allow it to fry whilst it over allowing both sides of the chicken to turn golden brown
  14. Using a colander spoon take out the fried chicken from the oil and place it aside on a plate
  15. Stir the rice and wait for a while after which you take it off the heat
  16. Serve the jollof rice on plate with the chicken with a cold drink and some salad

    17. In jollof there are nutrients such as vitamins from the vegetables, fats and oils from the cooking oil, protein from the chicken,minerals from the salt, carbohydrates from the rice, and water
