HOW TO GROW LONG AND HEALTHY HAIR

The hair is one of the most cherished part of the body. People spend a lot of time daily trying to make their hair look gorgeous.Some people have the urge to grow long and healthy hair which adds more beauty to their look on a daily basis.It is almost everyone's dream to grow long and healthy hair. This website will give you a step by step guidelines on how to grow long and healthy hair faster.

STEPS TO GROWING LONG AND HEALTHY HAIR

  1. Getting frequent trims
    Get your hair trimmed once split ends appear. This may seem like the wrong thing to do when you are trying to grow out your hair.Get your hair trimmed once you begin to see split ends and this can actually promote hair growth. Split ends come as dry, damaged and brittle parts of your hair and if they are not cut off can blend up with the strands of your hair. If you leave your split ends for a long period of time,you would have to cut a significant amount of your hair because the hair is somewhat damaged.
  2. Brushing your hair gently with usually with a boar bristle brush
    Using a boar bristle brush is a substitute for conditioning treatment.It is used to improve hair texture and shine.It also reduces the frizzy nature of the hair allowing the hair remain soft and moist.Furthermore it stimulates the scalp and this helps in promoting growth. Using the boar bristle brush improves blood flow to the hair follicles to stimulate hair growth.
  3. Shampooing your hair thrice a week
    Shampooing your hair thrice a week keeps your hair moist and shiny.Washing your hair everyday washes away essential oils in your hair which are very good for hair growth and also leaves your hair dry and frizzy. Avoiding washing your hair everyday will allow your hair to remain moisturised because it is not exposed to many chemicals that may be present in the shampoos we use on an everyday basis. Apart from just shampooing, conditioning your hair is also very important because it adds moisture to your hair and leaves it looking shiny and healhty.Choose good shampoos and conditioners for your hair which contain less chemicals and more of natural ingredients to further reduce hair breakage.
  4. Using silky pillows
    Using silky pillows instead of your normal cotton pillows help with hair growth by reducing bed head and friction onn your hair that causes hair damage such as split ends. We discussed the adverse effects of split ends earlier. Moreover silky pillows are easy to care for and are usually sold for a song on the market.
  5. Using hair mask at least once a week
    Hair masks add moisture and shine to your hair. Hair masks can be made from many items you have in your kitchen such as the eggs we eat everyday, mayonnaise, yoghurt, honey, olive oil and many other vital items.Using hair masks frequently will help make your hair look thicker and fuller as well as make it look moist and very supple.     Hair is made of protein, you need to eat a lot of protein foods such as eggs, meat, fish, milk and many other protein foods. This makes your hair strong and healthy but if you do not consume enough protein to promote the growth of healthy hair , your hair will end up becoming dry, brittle and weak.
