RECIPE FOR JOLLOF AND CHICKEN

INGREDIENTS
  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Pepper
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Chicken
  • Ginger
  • Maggi
  • Salt
  • Cucumber
  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Spring onion
  • Lettuce
  • Water

METHOD
  1. Put your saucepan on fire.
  2. Put your chopped chicken in the saucepan
  3. Add a reasonable amount of your grinded pepper and onion to the chicken.And sprinkle your maggi,salt and curry powder in a reasonable amount to spice up your chicken
  4. Add a reasonable amount of water to the spiced chicken on fire.
  5. Stir after the first 8 minutes.After 8 minutes your chicken will be well cooked
  6. Fry your chicken with oil
  7. Put your oil in another saucepan on fire
  8. Add your chopped onion and grinded pepper in it.
  9. Stir for a few minute
  10. Add your blended tomatoes
  11. Stir for it turns reddish brown.
  12. Add a reasonable amount of salt,maggi and curry powder.
  13. Add a reasonable amount of water and stir.Cover it.
  14. When it boils add the rice
  15. Add your chopped vegetables when your jollof is about cooked and stir.
  16. Wait for some few minutes then your dish is ready!
