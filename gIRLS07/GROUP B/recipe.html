HOW TO PREPARE RICE

Rice is very easy to cook . It is eaten by most people and it is the favourite food of most children.I am going to outline the procedures in cooking rice

ingredient:
  • rice
  • an appreciable amount of water depending on the amount of rice
  • salt
  • some small amount of oil if you prefere

method

method

method:
  1. wash your rice
  2. pour the oil in the sauce pan and put the sliced onion in the hot oil
  3. add the rice and pour water in it
  4. add salt
  5. stir gently
  6. wait for some few minutes
  7. check if it is ready to eat
  8. you are done
  9. thank you
