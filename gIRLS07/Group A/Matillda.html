how to make jollof rice

INGREDIENTS

  • tomato
  • pepper
  • salt
  • rice
  • water
  • tomato puree
  • chopped onions
  • oil

Method

Jollof is one of the best meals you can prepare with rice.To prepare jollof,you need the above ingredients.the method is as follows;

Pour oil into a saucepan and place on fire.Add your chopped onions and fry.Grind the tomatoes and add it to it.leave on fire for some time to remove the water and add tomato puree.add the pepper and salt.stir until stew is ready,addwaterand cover.when the water boils,add the rice,stir and cover.reducethe fire,wait for 20 more minutes and remove from fire.

you could also decide to add vegetables such as carrot,cucumber and spring onions after the water boils.

Now the jollof is ready.Garnish with salad.You can eat it with meat,egg or fish.

Nutritional Benefit

Jollof is a very nutritious food because even if you don't eat it with stew,you'll still have all the nutrients in their right proportions.
