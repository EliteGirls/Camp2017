Adolescence is period of life between childhood and adulthood.Adolescent reproductive health is a state of complete physical,social and mental wellbeing in all matters relating to the reproductive system and its functions and prossesses.

Reproductive rights are the rights of a couple to decide the number,spacing and timing of their children.

Reproductive rights are important because the help the couple to decide;
  • The number
  • the timing
  • The spacing of their children.
