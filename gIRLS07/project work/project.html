Grasshoppers are also isects.Some are dark brown while others are green.They have three pairs of legs.They have fore limbs which act as shock absorbers.They also havestrong and powerful hind wings which they use for jumping.The colour of grasshoppers give them a camouflage.Grasshoppers are diurnal.Which means they are active during the day and inactive during the night.

Grasshoppers also undergo incomplete metamorphosis.This mans their life cycle consist of three stages;the egg,nymph and adult.The nymph moults or undergoes ecdysis about seven times before it becomes an adult.

Adaptative Features Of The Grasshopper To Its Environment.

  • It has compound eyes for sight.
  • It has short forelimbs for walking and landing.
  • It has long and powerful hind limbs for jumping.
  • It has membranous fore wings for protection.
