SCULPTUREHOW TO MODEL THE BURST OF A HUMANBEING IN "IN-THE-ROUND RELIEF

The burst of a humanbeing means modelling from the head to the shoulder part of the human.Also,in-the-round relief in claywork means seeing the work from all angles.

Model Material

  1. clay
  2. Slabboard
  3. Sackboard
  4. Water

Model Tools

  1. The hand
  2. Frog(cutting wire)
  3. Bodkin
  4. Spatula
  5. Bodkin
  6. Foam

Procedures

  • Put down your idea development.
  • Grind your clay into powdery form.
  • Add a maximum amount of water to mix with clay to become plastic(sticky).
  • Knead the clay until it becomes leather-hard on the sackboard.
  • Use the frog to wedge the clay to check whether knead was done.When there are particles(pores) in it,it means knead is not done.
  • Make your slabboard with the thickness of 5inches.
  • When you are done,the clay you made for modelling,you start carving to get shape a humanbeings head.Doing that you start from the base.
  • Draw the eye on the clay you have mounted.
  • Start carving the eye from where you made your outline,bit by bit.
  • When you are done,do the same for the other features(the ears,the mouth,the forehead,the hair,the eyebrown.
  • Carve the up to get the neck to the shoulder.
  • Make sure that the head-length is equal to the length of the shoulder.
  • When you are done,check whether they are in order.
  • Use the foam to smoothen it.
  • This is end.Wash your tools for safety purpose.
