RECIPE FOR JOLLOF AND CHICKEN

INGREDIENTS

  1. Rice
  2. Tomato
  3. Oil
  4. Onion
  5. Spices
  6. Chicken
  7. Pepper
  8. Water
  9. Salt
  10. Vegetables

METHOD

  1. Heat the oil and fry the onion to golden brown
  2. Add your tomatoes, enough salt , pepper and spices and a little water and leave it to boil.
  3. Add enough water to cook the rice and leave it to boil for a while when the stew is almost cooked.
  4. Add your rice and stir
  5. Leave to boil.
  6. Make sure the heating is uniform .
  7. Cover the mouth of the saucepan with a clean with a clean napkin or cloth when the rice is almost cooked but still hard.
  8. Lower the heat and to cook.
  9. Steam your chikcken with preferred spices,salt and little water
    10. ,
  10. Take from fire when soft
  11. Drain the stock
  12. Heat enough oil in the frying pan for deep frying
  13. When hot gently place your steamed chicken in it
  14. Leave for awhile
  15. Ensure that the heat is uniform and medium
  16. Gently turn your chicken to the other side when brown and well fried
  17. Remove from the oil when both sides are well fried
  18. Place it in a strainer with tissue to absorb excess oil
  19. Remove the rice from fire when cooked
  20. Dish in a colourful plate
  21. Add your chicken
  22. Garnish with cut vegetables

Serve best when hot with a chilled cocktail drink
