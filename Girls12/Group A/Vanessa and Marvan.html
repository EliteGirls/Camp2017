Jollof

Go To Conclusion

Jollof is a very delicious food and it is also nutritious

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Rice
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Spices(black pepper and curry powder)
  • Corned Beef
  • Vegetables

Recipe

  1. Put the saucepan on fire
  2. Pour oil into the saucepan
  3. Slice onions and put it into the oiland fry
  4. Pour the tomato paste inside the saucepan and stir
  5. Add your spices and taste to make sure it is in its right quantity
  6. Add your rice after some few minutes
  7. Add corned beef
  8. Stir
  9. Add water and stir
  10. Cover the saucepan and wait for some minutes
  11. Stir from time to time to prevent burning
  12. Add water if the water was not enough and stir
  13. Wait for some minutes.
  14. It is cooked when it is soft and there is no water in it
  15. Take it off the fire.

It is best to serve it hot with your cut vegetables

Nutrient

Many nutriens are gotten from jollof. A few are

  • Protein
  • Carbohydrates
  • Vitamins and Minerals

Follow the above step and you will enjoy your meal.
