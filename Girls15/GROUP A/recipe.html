Method to prepare jollof

Go to bottom

Ingredients

  • sliced tomatoes
  • sliced onion
  • spices
  • powdered pepper
  • oil
  • fish
  • 1/2teaspoon salt
  • tomato paste
  • 2cups of rice

Instructions/Recipe

    Get a clean saucepan
    Put your saucepan on fire
    pour oil in the saucepan
    Add your sliced onion and tomatoes
    Add the tomatoe paste and salt
    wait for about 3minutes
    put the fishes and stir gently
    add the powdered pepper and the spices
    add waterand wait for about 3 minutes for the water to boil
    then finally add your 2 cups of rice
