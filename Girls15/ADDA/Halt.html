HOW TO MODEL A HOUSE IN THREE-DIMENSION

Modelling is the first start clay work

  • Grind the clay
  • Add water to the clay you grinded to become plastic
  • Knead the plastic clay to become leather-hard on onended=""sackboard
  • Prepare your slap with a thickness of 5mm
  • Remove the slap from the sackboard and put it on a board
  • Draw your house on the slap you made
  • Start modelling bit by bit until you get want you want
  • Smoothen it when you are done until you get the appropriate shape
  • Wash your tools with water to clear the clay on it.
