PLANTAIN

Plantain is a type of bananas. it falls into three groups, namely; banana,plantain and ornamental. the most common and fast growing plantain is the musa plantain.
plantains are suckers and are a giant clumping tropical herb. Plantains are less-sweet, starchier equivalent to the banana.
they are almost always cooked before eating and they taste pretty awful raw.
