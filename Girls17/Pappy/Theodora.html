Science

Science comes in various fields. They are;Physics, Biology and Chemistry.

Definition to the various fields in science:

  • Physics:
    • this is the scientific study of matter and its motion as well as time and space.
  • Biology:
    • this is the scientific study of the forms and functions of living organisms and their interrelationship with the environments.
  • Chemistry:
    • this is the scientific study of the chemical composition that forms chemical reaction between energy and forces.

Scientific Method:
This is the systematic approach used by scientist to solve problems.
