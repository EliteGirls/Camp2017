Introduction to Biology

Cell

A cell is the basic unit from which all living things are built.Each cell is capable of carrying out life processes.All cells arise from pre-existing cells by the process of cell division.

There are two types of Cells.They are:

  • Unicellular Cells:
    these are living things that consists of only one living cell.Examp:amoeba.
  • Multicellular Cells:
    these are living things containing many cells.Example:humanbeings ,prokaryotae.

Prokaryotes and Akaryotes

Akaryotes are not strictly cells but particles.All viruses are akaryotes.They differ from true cells,eukaryotes,in that they do not have a nucleus,cytoplasm or cell membrane whiles Prokaryotes differ from other cells in that,they have no definite nucleus or cytoplasm.They include bacteria.Prokaryotes are unicellular organisms with a simple internal structure.
