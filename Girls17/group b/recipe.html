HOW TO PREPARE RICE

rice is easy to cook

INGREDIENTS

  • rice
  • pepper
  • seasoning
  • fish
  • onion
  • tomato

METHOD

  1. place your saucepan on the fire
  2. pour an ample amount of water in the saucepan,wait for about five minutes for the water to be hot
  3. now,pour the rice in the water
  4. wait for about seven minutes,your rice is ready for consuming
