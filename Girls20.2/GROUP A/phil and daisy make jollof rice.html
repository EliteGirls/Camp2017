Preparing jollof rice

go to conclusion

  • Ingredients
    • rice
    • pepper
    • onion
    • oil
    • tomatoes
    • spices
    • meat
    • water
    • salt

Recipe/instruction

  • first cut and wash the meat
  • place meat in sauce pan add chopped onions and spices and place it on fire to steam for five minutes
  • after steaming drain sauce from the meat
  • pour oil into sauce pan and place on fire
  • put the meat into the oil when is hot, fry until golden brown and drain the oil from the meat
  • wash the pepper,onion,tomatoes,garlic and ginger add a little amount of water and blend
  • pour a little amount of oil into saucepan to start stew
  • put your chopped onions in the oil and after a minute add the blended ingredients ie.the onions,tomatoes etc
  • allow to boil for five minutes and some salt and spices to taste
  • allow to boil for alonger period of time
  • wash the rice thoroughly,drain water and add some amount of water to the stew and allow to boil again for some time
  • put the rice into the boiling stew,stir and allow it to cook for some time and add fried meat
  • NB.you have to keep stiring it regularly to prevent the rice from burning
  • serve with shito

Nutrients/benefits

  • it contains nutrients such as proteins and carbohydrates which regulates body growth
