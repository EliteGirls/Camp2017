Some forms of technology are:

  1. Mobile Phones
  2. Cars
  3. Cookers
  4. Irons
  5. Computers

Benefits Of Technology

  • Makes work faster
  • Saves time
  • Easy access to internet
  • Increases productivity
Disadvatages Of Technology
