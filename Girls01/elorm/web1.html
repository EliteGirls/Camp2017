ABOUT CELESTINA.

Some where in the land of the west African part of the world is
found a country called GHANA. In this country is the region called VOLTA
with a portion of its land called Tsiame and this is place where I
Celestina Fugah hail from. I was raised by the Mr.
Fugah Daniel and Miss Adetu Elizabeth family where they are both ewes
and my mother is from Dzita and father is from Tsiame. The ewes practice
the patrilineal heritage so I am also from Tsiame.

I am girl of great ambitions
and I listen to voices around me and see what I can do about they think of me.
I am a proud science student of the school called
UNIVERSITY PRACTICE SENIOR HIGH and I am in form three.
