Accra High School

Accra high school later was fully granted by the government of ghana in 1923.It started with only seven(7) student.Later on,around 1942,Accra high school became a popular school with about 5000 student.Accra High school won the best senior high school in 1976.It is accademically a good school with brilliant student.Accra High school won the national science and maths quiz(NSMQ) in 1999 during L.T. J.J RAWLING'S time. Accra high school is noted as a fully science school.It has a well comfortable science lab with all the equipment,apparatus and technogy.Accra high school is one of the best school in greater accra.Accra high school always perform accreditably in wassce.It would have been a first class school but it lack boarding facilities.it is a fully day school with hostels for only form 3 student./p>
