Teenage Pregnancy

One social problem that is increasing rampantly in Africa especially Ghana is Teenage Pregnancy.

WHAT IS TEENAGE PREGNANCY????

Teenage Pregnancy occurs when a girl between the ages of 11-19 gets pregnant.

Year after year cases of teenage pregnancy is recorded especially in the rural areas

CAUSES OF TEENAGE PREGNANCY

  • poverty;because of the unavailability of social amenities, most teens engage themselves in risky and immoral activities such as prostitution just to earn money.
  • peer pressure;most peers are influenced by friends to engage themselves in prostitution most innocent girls fall victim and become pregnant at a tender age.
